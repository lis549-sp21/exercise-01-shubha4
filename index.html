Elizabeth Bennet

Elizabeth is the second-youngest daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Bennet. She is an intelligent young woman who questions the societal norms of the 1800s-England.

She is a character from Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen.

Reasons why I admire Elizabeth Bennet:

  • She is very witty and smart.
  • She loves her family and is willing to do anything for them.
  • She believes that a woman's value extends beyond her ability to produce children.
